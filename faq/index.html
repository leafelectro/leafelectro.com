Frequently Asked Questions

What DAW, synths, plugins are you using?

Ableton Live on OSX. I resample wavetables from various synths and loop them through a sampler to avoid using any third-party plugin. The only plugins I use are iZotope Ozone's Imager for stereo width, ValhallaRoom for reverb, and A.O.M's Invisible Limiter.

Can I use or promote your music on my YouTube channel?

Yes you can, as long as you mention where the music comes from, or don't try to make it look like you made it. Ideally, have a link to my SoundCloud page or to this site in your description, but at the very least make sure "Leaf - [song name]" is written somewhere.

Can I remix your songs?

I would love that! On the Music section of this site, you will find links to download remix stems, MIDI files, and screenshots, to help you getting started with your remix.

Can we collab? Can you share my track/mix?

As much as I love discovering new musicians, I am not planning on doing any collaboration work at the moment, as I'm still trying to find my own style. I also won't share your tracks on my SoundCloud page, that would certainly bother my followers!

What's up with all these ads?

The reason you've heard about me might be because you've seen an ad / sponsored link somewhere. Getting heard in this saturated field is difficult and ads are one of the communication media I've chosen to get my music to your ears. I apologize if they bothered you, and would recommend installing AdBlock in your browser to hide them.
